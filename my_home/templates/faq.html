MyVault FAQ

MyVault is a secure platform where you can store your money safely. It provides you with a unique private key for account access and enables seamless transactions between users.

You can access your MyVault account using your unique private key, which is generated upon registration. It's essential to keep this key safe, as it cannot be restored or reset by anyone, including the administrators.

Your private key is displayed only once during the initial setup in a warning window. After that, it is not stored anywhere, so you must keep it safe and secure. If you lose it, you will not be able to access your account.

You can top up your MyVault account using Visa or Mastercard. This allows you to add funds to your account conveniently.

Yes! You can send money between MyVault users for free using unique sending and receiving addresses created during registration.

You can access your transaction history from the transactions page in your account. To view this information, you will need to validate your private key.

In your profile, you can activate or disable email notifications for logins and manage your login location settings.

If you forget your password, you can restore it using your private key. This emphasizes the importance of keeping your private key secure.

Your private key is not stored in any database, which means that neither the admin nor an attacker can access it. It is your responsibility to keep it safe.

No, sending money between MyVault users is completely free. You can enjoy seamless transactions without any additional costs.
